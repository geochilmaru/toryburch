DESCRIPTION

Color:

Named for Tory’s parents — Buddy and Reva Robinson — it’s a modern classic. The epitome of understated chic, our new Robinson Convertible Shoulder Bag is made of the highest-quality, scratch-resistant leather. The chain strap is adjustable, so it can be worn doubled or long and cross-body — perfect for days on the go or evenings out.

DETAILS & FIT

  • Holds a continental wallet, an iPhone 6 Plus and a lip color
  • Saffiano leather
  • Flap with magnetic snap closure
  • Adjustable, leather-and-chain strap with 23.3" (58.5 cm) drop
  • 1 exterior slide pocket under front flap
  • 1 interior zipper pocket, 2 open pockets
  • Can be worn as a shoulder bag or cross-body
  • Height: 6.37" (16 cm)
  • Length: 8.57" (21.5 cm)
  • Depth: 3.98" (10 cm)
